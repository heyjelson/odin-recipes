Casava Cake Recipe

The delicious Cassava Cake.

Everyone's favorite!

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 kilo Cassava Flesh
  • 1/2 cup Water
  • 3 Egg
  • 1 can(390 grams) Condensed Milk and 400 grams Evap Milk
  • 1/2 cup Coco Milk
  • 1 spoonfli Sugar
  • 1 pack 300 grams Cheese
  • 1/2 cup Butter

Steps:

  1. First, set aside 90 grams of Condensed Milk, 100 grams of Evap Milk, and 200 grams of Cheese for toppings.
  2. Mix well all the remaining ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Put mixed ingredients in a butter greased cooking pan.
  4. Put the pan in 350 degrees hot Oven for 30 minutes.
  5. Mixed the set aside Condensed, Evap Milk, and Cheese in a bowl.
  6. After 30 minutes check-out the pan in the oven and filled it with the mixed toppings.
  7. Put it again in the Oven for another 15 minutes.
  8. That's it! Taste your Creamy and Chessy Cassava Cake.
